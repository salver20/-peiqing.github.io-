About me

Currently I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Jikai Liu. I received my B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees from the School of Software engineering and the School of Computer Science and Technology at Shandong University in 2014 and 2019, respectively. My research interests include Computer graphics, Computer aided design, Computational fabrication, Topology optimization, Geometric modeling and Additive manufacturing.
