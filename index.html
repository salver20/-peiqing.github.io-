About me

Currently I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Shandong University, supervised by Prof. Jikai Liu.

I got my B.Eng. degree from the School of Software engineering at Shandong University in 2018 and received M.Eng. degree at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Lin Lu. My research interests include Computer aided design, Computational fabrication and Topology optimization.
